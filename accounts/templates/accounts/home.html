The repair problem

Home repair needs more than a phone call and guesswork.

Customers need one place to describe a problem and see what happened next. Professionals need a clean queue instead of scattered messages and unclear job status.

Requests become organized records.

A customer can save the title, description, category, urgency, and address for each repair request.

Professionals get a clear pipeline.

Professional accounts can review open requests, accept work, and manage assigned requests.

Status stays visible.

Open, In Progress, Completed, and Cancelled states keep the repair record understandable.

How it works

A simple repair workflow with real account roles.

UstoHub Lite focuses on the core marketplace path already supported by the project.

  1. 1

    Customer posts a Repair Request

    The customer describes the issue, chooses a category, sets urgency, and adds the service address.

  2. 2

    Professional accepts the request

    A professional account can view open requests and accept available work from the queue.

  3. 3

    Customer tracks progress

    The request status shows whether the repair is open, in progress, completed, or cancelled.

  4. 4

    Work is completed

    The professional marks accepted work completed, and the record stays in the completed queue.

Repair categories

Electrical

Appliance

Other
